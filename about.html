The Handy Couple Story

HallAway grew from years of hands-on work by Mike and Judy, known by many neighbors as the Handy Couple.

Our Mission

We believe every homeowner and property owner should have one trusted call for repairs, cleanup, and practical help. HallAway reflects that mission: move problems out of your way and keep your space working.

From Hallway to HallAway

As our services expanded from handyman tasks into full hauling and cleanup support, we transitioned to the HallAway name to better reflect what we do every day in the Canton community.

Experience You Can Count On

Our skill set spans painting, decorating, small repairs, auto repair, junk removal, deliveries, and yard clearing. That range means fewer calls for you and faster results for your home or property.
